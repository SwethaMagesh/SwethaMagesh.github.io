My Interests

My hobbies and interests. I guess interests come and go. We get crazy about certain things at certain periods of our life. I will update my latest crazes here.

Novels, History and Mythology

I love history and mythology - especially epics and stories with too many twists in the plot. Probably that is why I read a lot of novels. I have recently been looking up World Wars, Cold Wars, Vietnam and Korean Wars and more about countries like Russia where mysteries are said to exist. The reason was Jeffrey Archer's novels - primarily the Clifton Chronicles and Heads You Win. The YouTube channel Ted-Ed also inspires me. There are countless historical and mythological videos, but its duration is less than five minutes- just what I want. I was also looking up Plagues of 1800's due to the preveiling Corona Pandemic. Somehow, these events far beyond, make me wonder hypothetically what would have happened had I been there.

Secrets and Codes

I love secrets, codes and breaking them. Like recently I am addicted to Cryptography, Encryption and Decryption and stuff. Whenever I see the names of people who broke a RSA number into two or found the largest prime, I simply wonder if I could be one of them one day.
